Rock Climbing

Without doubt one of the most unique experiences in the world - high quality, technical, rock climbing high above a major city...with ocean views as well! Besides the integral-ascent all other options include abseiling. Note that all routes on Table Mountain are Traditional (no bolts), and our guides are certified to this level in all cases.

Fishing

Here you can try for galjoen and steenbras in front of the caravan park and the river mouth, using prawn and mussel. Fly fish for small leeries on the vlei.For beach fishing, walk along the Main Beach to the High Dune; fish from the rocks along the shoreline at Sandown Bay, you may get the odd galjoen in season.
